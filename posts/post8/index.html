As I approach my final semester at CofC, the anticipation of earning my first bachelor’s degree fills me with immense pride and joy. Plans for the Semester:

In the coming months, I am set to embark on a journey that encompasses the final leg of my undergraduate education. My focus will be on completing four essential courses that will not only bring me closer to graduation but also expand my knowledge base significantly.

Programming Concept Language: This course will further deepen my understanding of programming concepts, providing valuable insights into the world of coding and problem-solving.

Operating Systems: Exploring the intricacies of operating systems will equip me with a comprehensive understanding of how computers function at the core, enhancing my technical skills.

Database Concepts: Building a solid foundation in database concepts is crucial in today’s data-driven world. This course will enable me to work with data efficiently and effectively.

Linear Algebra: Gaining proficiency in linear algebra will refine my mathematical skills and contribute to a well-rounded education.

Additionally, this Fall semester promises an exciting opportunity as I step into the role of a Research Assistant Tester for Cirdles.org under the guidance of Dr. Bowring. This role will not only allow me to apply my academic knowledge but also contribute to meaningful research and development endeavors.

As my academic journey nears its conclusion, I am excited to embrace the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead. Stay tuned for further updates as I progress through this semester, where each day brings me closer to the fulfillment of my undergraduate dreams and the beginning of a promising future.
